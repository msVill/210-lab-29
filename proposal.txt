Project Overview:
My program will simulate changes that cities go through over a 
50-year time period. The simulation will depict the impact of job
availability, housing conditions, and population trends within three
different cities in the same state.

Implementation:
I will use an std::mao where each key represents a city name. The value
will be a 3-element std::array of std::list. These three lists will
represent:
- Job availability
- Housing conditions
- Population increase or decrease.
The program will read at least 100 lines of data from an external file
to initialize the map. Each line will contain a city name, a category
(job, housing, or population), and a value, which will be inserted into
the appropriate list.

Simulated Events:
The simulation will run 50 time periods (years). During each time
period:
- Population may increase/decrease in each city.
- Job availability may improve or decline, affecting economic conditions
- Housing availability may increase or decrease, influencing affordability
and potential homelessness.
These changes may also indirectly affect crime rates within each city.
